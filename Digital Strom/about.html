Company Profile

Digital Storm has been in business since 2002, in that time its reputation as a premiere system integrator has grown exponentially. Digital Storm's driving philosophy is to deliver to its customers cutting-edge technology and performance along with rock-solid support. As a validation of Digital Storm's success the company has retained an "A+" rating from the Better Business Bureau. Digital Storm produces some of the fastest gaming computers in the market. It is constantly pushing the boundaries of performance so that its customers will enjoy a gaming experience unmatched by any of Digital Storm's competitors.

As testament to Digital Storm's acumen, it has won numerous awards from industry publications like Maximum PC's "Kick Ass" Award, PC Gamer Magazine's "Editor's Choice" Award, Anandtech.com's "Silver Editor's Choice" Award, PC Perspective's "Gold Award", and many others.

Digital Storm works with its channel partners to help build strong brand recognition. Digital Storm is always marketing and advertising its preference for only the highest quality parts and products.

Having a quality product, stellar customer service, and a superior reputation has allowed Digital Storm to grow every year since 2002, starting out in a garage to now occupying a 27,000 Sq. ft. facility. Digital Storm's management and employees are convinced that not even the current economic downturn can derail the company's growth and increasing market share.
